ShadowPocket Support

Support information for the ShadowPocket iOS app.

Contact

For bug reports, connection issues, or app feedback, please open an issue on GitHub: https://github.com/mol32/ShadowPocket/issues

Before reporting an issue

  • Confirm that the proxy or VPN server configuration is valid.
  • Confirm that the server is reachable from your network.
  • Include the app version, iOS version, and a short description of the problem.
  • Do not post passwords, tokens, private keys, subscription URLs, or full server credentials.

Privacy

The app privacy policy is available at ShadowPocket Privacy Policy.
