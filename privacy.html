ShadowPocket Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

ShadowPocket is a VPN and proxy client. The app lets users add, import, and use proxy or VPN server configurations on their own devices.

Information We Collect

ShadowPocket does not collect personal information, create user accounts, use advertising identifiers, or include third-party analytics SDKs.

The app does not upload your proxy configurations, subscription URLs, server credentials, browsing history, DNS queries, or network traffic contents to the developer.

Local Data

Proxy and VPN configurations that you add to the app are stored locally on your device. Diagnostic information shown inside the app is intended to help you troubleshoot local connection problems.

You should not share passwords, tokens, private keys, subscription URLs, or full server credentials when requesting support.

Network Connections

When you connect through ShadowPocket, your device may communicate with the proxy or VPN servers that you configure. ShadowPocket does not operate those servers. The operators of servers or subscription services that you choose may process information according to their own policies.

Apple Services

If you install ShadowPocket through TestFlight or the App Store, Apple may process diagnostics, crash reports, and purchase or download records according to Apple's own privacy policies and account settings.

Children's Privacy

ShadowPocket is not intended to collect information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the app changes. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, please use the project support page: ShadowPocket Support.
